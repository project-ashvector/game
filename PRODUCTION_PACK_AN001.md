# Production Pack 005 — AN-001 Ashborn Revenant

## Status
Integrated in Developer Build v13.

## Purpose
AN-001 is the first standard Anomaly in the vertical slice. It is the baseline enemy used to test encounter flow, battle UI, loot rewards, anomaly database entries, and future monster asset standards.

## Gameplay Role
- Low-threat aggressive melee Anomaly
- Introduces basic combat
- Drops early crafting and upgrade materials
- Used in Fracture 001: Toxic Sewers

## Design Notes
- Shape language: canine silhouette, jagged metal spine, exposed red energy glow
- Palette: black iron, rust red, ash gray, toxic sewer highlights
- Weaknesses: Fire / Energy
- Resistance: Physical
